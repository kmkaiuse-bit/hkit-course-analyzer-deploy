üîÑ How The System Works

Step-by-Step Process

  1. Upload: Staff uploads student's transcript PDF and enters student information
  2. Analysis: AI analyzes transcript courses against HKIT programme requirements
  3. Review: Staff reviews AI suggestions and can edit any recommendations
  4. Confirm: Staff confirms results and can save to database for learning purposes
  5. Export: Generate reports in CSV, Excel, or PDF format for records
